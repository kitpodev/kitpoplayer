3. Permissions Explained

Our app requests the following permissions, which are used ONLY for the stated purposes:

3.1 Storage/Media Access Permissions

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below): Required to read audio files from your device
  • READ_MEDIA_AUDIO (Android 13+): Required to access music files on newer Android versions

Purpose: To display and play music files stored on your device. Without this permission, the app cannot function as a music player.

3.2 Foreground Service Permissions

  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Allows music playback to continue when app is in background
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK: Specific permission for media playback service

Purpose: To keep playing music when you switch to other apps or lock your screen.

3.3 Notification Permission

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Required to display playback controls in notifications

Purpose: To show music controls (play, pause, next, previous) in your notification area for convenient access.

3.4 Wake Lock Permission

  • WAKE_LOCK: Prevents device from sleeping during playback

Purpose: To ensure uninterrupted music playback.

3.5 Permissions We Explicitly BLOCK

  • ‚ùå ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE - We don't need internet connectivity information
